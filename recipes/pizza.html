Margherita Pizza

Description:

This is a simple yet tasteful pizza that primarily has tomato sauce, cheese, and basil.

Ingredients:

  • 12 inch pizza crust or Italian bread shell
  • 3 plum tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1/3 Buitoni Refrigerated Pesto with Basil
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F. Place pizza crust on baking sheet.
  2. Spread pesto over pizza crust.
  3. Arrange tomatoes over pesto and sprinkle with cheese and crushed red pepper.
  4. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.
  5. Cut into slices for the family.
  6. Enjoy!
