Avocado Smoothie

Description:

This avocado smoothie recipe is a great way to battle the heat!

This smoothie features creamy avocados.

Ingredients:

  • Avocados
  • Condensed milk
  • Ice
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Split the avocado into halves and remove the pit. Scoop contents into blender.
  2. Add milk, condensed milk, ice, and the avocado into the blender.
  3. Enjoy!
